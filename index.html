Abstract

Single-image 3D reconstruction remains a challenging task due to geometric ambiguity and texture inconsistencies. Existing methods often suffer from distorted shapes or blurred details. We present GeCo-Diff, an automated end-to-end pipeline that leverages diffusion priors for both view synthesis and geometry estimation. By enforcing orbital consistency and enhancing texture details via Real-ESRGAN and VGGT, our method enables robust 3D Gaussian Splatting training without manual intervention.

Method Overview


Our pipeline consists of four key stages: 1) Video Generation using SV3D, 2) Refinement with Frame filtering and Super-Resolution, 3) Geometry Estimation via VGGT foundation model, and 4) 3D Reconstruction using Gaussian Splatting with background regularization.
